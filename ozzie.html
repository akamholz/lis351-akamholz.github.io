My Dog

This is my dog Ozzie! He is a Havanese dog that we got in early 2018. His name comes from the Chicago White Sox manager Ozzie Guillen, who led them to a World Series win in 2005. This picture is him as a puppy.

Ozzie likes to chew on bottle caps!

Ozzie likes to sit weirdly on occasion.

Ozzie really likes human food. Whenever someone is eating, he likes to beg them for their food. This is Ozzie begging me for some of my lunch.
